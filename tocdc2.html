Changing to CDC Risk Levels

You are now switching to a different risk level set. These new levels, the CDC Transmission Indicators, use the same metrics as the MI Start Map Levels (% positive tests and case rates) but calculates them differently.

Risk Level Calculation

MIStartMap Risk Levels

Uses the worse indicator of:

  • the 7-day average number of new cases per million population per day, OR
  • the 7-day average percent positivity

Risk Level Calculation

CDC Risk Levels

Uses the worse indicator of:

  • the total number of new cases per 100,000 population over the previous 7 days, OR
  • the 7-day average percent positivity
